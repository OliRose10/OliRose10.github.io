Who I Am

About Rose Above

ICAEW ACA Part-Qualified

Accountancy for musicians, indie labels, creatives, and ambitious entrepreneurs.
Hi! I'm Oliver Rose, ICAEW ACA part-qualified accountant. With four years of finance experience and a lifelong passion for music, I founded Rose Above to help independent artists, bands, managers, and labels get clarity on their finances, grow sustainably, and stay focused on what they do best: making great music.
I offer clarity, confidence, and practical support for your unique journey, from everyday tax to big tours.

How I Can Help

Services

Bookkeeping & Tax Returns

Tour & Project Budgets & Forecasts
