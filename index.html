About Me

Hi, I’m Oliver Rose (he/him), a 25-year-old finance professional based in the UK.
Currently: ACA candidate, working towards my ICAEW ACA qualification.
Day job: Absolute Label Services (Music Industry), supporting independent artists and labels.
Background: Music lover, spreadsheet enthusiast, and passionate about helping creatives thrive.
Freelance Status: Just starting my journey—join me at the very beginning and let’s grow together!

My Mission

  • Make finance and tax simple for creatives.
  • Bring indie values and empathy to financial support.
  • Offer affordable, jargon-free advice for artists at any stage.
  • Build long-term relationships as I grow my own freelance practice.
